Planetary Cataclysm


Welcome to Planetary Cataclysm! This is a simulation about the n-body problem and chaos theory. The simulation is of our solar system and is written in Mathematica. Your objective is to destabilize our solar system. You do this by changing the masses of the planets through the sliders in the app. To run the simulation, press the run button. Try out different combinations of masses to see their effect! For more information about the n-body problem, please go to this link: n-body Problem.
